How it works

Every email handled. Every time.

OTW TriageAI reads your inbox around the clock, sorts what matters, and drafts replies — so you only deal with what needs a human touch.

01
Email arrives

Every inbound email hits your inbox as normal. OTW TriageAI reads it within seconds.
