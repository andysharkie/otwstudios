FAQ

Common questions

Will callers know they're talking to an AI?

Yes — the assistant is transparent that it's an AI receptionist. It doesn't pretend to be human. Most callers don't care as long as their call is handled quickly and professionally.

What happens if the AI can't handle a call?

For anything outside its scope, the assistant takes the caller's details, commits to a callback, and notifies you immediately. Nothing is dropped.

How long does setup take?

Core plan: 48 hours from sale to live. Full Service: 72 hours. We handle all the setup — you just review and approve before anything goes live.

Do I need to set up call forwarding?

No forwarding needed. Each client gets a dedicated OTW phone number. You update your Google Business listing to that number — the AI answers directly. Simple.

Does it work for my industry?

Currently configured for plumbing, electrical, HVAC, and carpentry. Other trades are being onboarded — contact us to check your industry.
