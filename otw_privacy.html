Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Andy Ngo t/as OTW Studios

Who we are

OTW Studios is a workflow automation service for trade businesses, operated by Andy Ngo t/as OTW Studios (ABN to be published upon registration). We provide AI-powered tools including an AI Receptionist, email triage automation, and a free Business Audit calculator.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at andy@otwstudios.com.au. We aim to respond within 5 business days.

What information we collect

Information you provide directly

  • Contact details — your name, email address, and phone number
  • Business information — business name, trade or industry, and location
  • Operational information — team size, call volumes, email habits, scheduling methods, tools currently in use, technology comfort level, monthly budget range, and goals. This information is provided through our free Business Audit tool.

Information collected automatically

  • Website usage data collected via Google Analytics — including pages visited, time spent on each page, browser type, device type, and approximate geographic location. This data is aggregated and anonymised; it does not identify you personally.
  • Cookies used by Google Analytics to distinguish unique visitors and track sessions. No personally identifiable information is stored in these cookies.

Why we collect it

We use your information to:

  • Generate your personalised Business Audit report and deliver it to your nominated email address
  • Notify us that a new audit has been completed so we can follow up promptly
  • Contact you about OTW Studios services that are relevant to your business situation
  • Send occasional follow-up communications — you can opt out at any time by replying "unsubscribe"
  • Improve and optimise our website, audit tool, and service offering

We will not use your information for any purpose not listed above without first obtaining your consent. We do not use your data for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

How long we keep your data

We retain personal information collected through our Business Audit for up to 24 months from the date it was submitted. After this period, it is securely deleted. If you ask us to delete your information before that date, we will do so within 10 business days.

Anonymised, aggregated analytics data (Google Analytics) has no retention limit as it cannot identify you.

Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct any information that is inaccurate, incomplete, or out of date
  • Ask us to delete your personal information (subject to any legal obligations we may have to retain it)
  • Opt out of marketing or follow-up communications at any time
  • Complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) if you believe we have mishandled your data

To exercise any of these rights, email andy@otwstudios.com.au with "Privacy Request" in the subject line. We will respond within 5 business days.

Cookies and tracking

Our website uses Google Analytics, which places cookies on your device to collect anonymised usage data. No personally identifiable information is shared with Google through this process.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. You can also manage or delete cookies through your browser settings — note that doing so may affect how some parts of our website function.

We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting cookies, or any cookies for profiling purposes.

Data security

We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to protect your personal information from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. All data transmitted through our website uses HTTPS encryption. Our third-party service providers maintain their own security controls in accordance with industry standards.

In the unlikely event of a data breach that is likely to result in serious harm, we will notify affected individuals and the OAIC in accordance with the Notifiable Data Breaches (NDB) scheme.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time as our services evolve. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our site or services after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. We will notify existing contacts of any material changes by email.
